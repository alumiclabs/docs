Every piece of authored content in Alumic is an **entity** — a typed row in a project-scoped database with `data`, `editor_metadata`, `tags`, and a monotonic `version`. Characters, dialogues, locations, scenes, items, abilities, UI layouts, canvas boards, and issues are all entity types.
